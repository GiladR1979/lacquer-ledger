Lacquer Ledger support

How can we help?

For help not covered below, email g_ronen@yahoo.com. Include your iOS version and a short description of what happened. Do not include sensitive information.

Adding a bottle

Open the Add tab, enter the brand and shade name, choose whether it belongs on your shelf or wishlist, and tap Save polish. Finish, color family, notes, and a photo are optional.

Duplicate warnings

Lacquer Ledger compares brand and shade names without treating capitalization or surrounding spaces as different. If the same pair already exists, the app warns before another record is created.

Recording a wear

On your shelf, tap Wearing today on the bottle you chose. The Wear tab shows its updated count and recent-wear position.

Exporting your collection

Tap Export above your collection. Lacquer Ledger creates a CSV file and opens the iOS share sheet. Save it to Files, send it to yourself, or open it in a spreadsheet app.

Photos and permissions

Photo-library access is optional and requested only when you tap Choose photo. Selecting a photo copies it into Lacquer Ledger's private app storage. The app does not upload it.

Backing up and restoring

CSV export provides a portable copy of your bottle details and wear fields. Version 1.0 does not import CSV files or sync between devices.

Deleting data

Tap Remove on a bottle and confirm. Deleting the entire app removes its local database and app-managed photos, subject to standard iOS backup and restore behavior.

Privacy

No account, analytics, advertising, or remote server is used. Read the full privacy policy.
