Where is my collection stored?

In a local database inside the app on your iPhone. Lacquer Ledger does not operate a server.

Can I back up my collection?

Yes. Export your collection as a CSV file and save it wherever you choose through the iOS share sheet.

Is there a subscription?

No. Lacquer Ledger is a one-time purchase.
