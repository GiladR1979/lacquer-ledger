Privacy Policy

Lacquer Ledger is designed to be private and offline.

Data collection

Lacquer Ledger does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, accounts, or a remote server.

Data stored on your device

Bottle details, wishlist entries, notes, color and finish selections, and wear history are stored in the app's local database on your device.

If you choose a bottle photo, Lacquer Ledger copies that selected image into the app's private local storage. The app does not upload the photo. Photo-library access is requested only after you choose the photo feature.

Exporting data

When you choose Export, Lacquer Ledger creates a CSV file in temporary app storage and opens the iOS share sheet. You control whether and where that file is shared. Lacquer Ledger does not receive information about your chosen destination.

Deleting data

You can remove individual bottles inside the app. Deleting the app removes its local database and app-managed photos from the device, subject to standard iOS backup and restore behavior.

Children

Lacquer Ledger is a general-audience utility and does not knowingly collect information from children or anyone else.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated policy and revision date will be published on this page.
